About The Club

THE UNIVERSITY CLUB IS MORE THAN AN ARCHITECTURAL LANDMARK.

Through the vitality of its members, it has evolved into New York’s premier social club offering unparalleled services to members - from gracious dining, luxurious accommodations and a variety of social events, to extensive athletic facilities and the world’s largest and finest club library.

Steeped in tradition, the club reflects a warm and welcoming ambiance, while addressing the current needs of its membership. Enthusiastic member volunteers commit theirskills and time to create this appealing atmosphere. Over the years, through strong and active committees, members have been the source of the inspiration and leadership that ensures the club’s continued relevance and importance to their lives.

Members are the club’s heart and soul.
