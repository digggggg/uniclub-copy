Guest Room

ROOMS & SUITES

The University Club is conveniently located in the heart of New York’s shopping, financial and cultural districts. For the exclusive benefit of our members and their sponsored guests, we have 97 overnight rooms. Each of our elegantly appointed overnight rooms is designed to accommodate your every need. You and your guests will enjoy all the pleasures and privileges of our private club. In addition, you will enjoy luxurious comfort, fine cuisine and wine, state-of-the-art athletic and squash facilities and our premier Library. From the moment you arrive, our goal is to provide you with personal service of the highest standard. Consider the clubhouse your home-away-from-home during your stay.

The Club’s overnight rooms are elegantly appointed featuring Chippendale-style furniture, luxury linens, period-style bathrooms and specially-selected artwork. Overnight guest rooms enjoy soft, comfortable robes, luxurious cotton towels and signature bath amenities. In room high-speed wireless internet access keeps you connected to colleagues, family and friends. Room service, valet, fully-stocked mini bars, complimentary coffee maker, perfect for an overnight experience. Additional amenities include 55" flat screen TV with cable, AM/FM Clock Radio, full-length mirrors, wake-up service, hair dryers, iron and board, in-room safes, theater ticket and car and limo service, reduced parking rates. Roll-away beds are also available upon request.

University Club Junior and Deluxe suites offer spacious and luxurious accommodations. In addition to what is offered in our standard rooms, the clubs junior suites offer a living room area,flat screen TV’s and our deluxe suites offer a living area and dining table for four, with king-size beds and flat screen TV’s. All our comfortable sofas feature a pull-out bed.

Our professional staff will work hard to ensure that when you are here, you feel right at home. Your comfort remains our highest priority. For reservations, kindly click here. Or you can call our hotel Front Desk staff at 212-572-3415/16.
